Welcome to BCM Open 2025

Welcome to the BCM Open 2025 Chess Championship!

Gather with us from November 20–23, 2025, at the iconic Mulungushi International Conference Centre in central Lusaka, conveniently located on Great East Road and easily accessible from the city center, airport, and major transportation routes.

Organized by BCM Consult, this premier FIDE-rated tournament promises a competitive experience wrapped in comfort, professionalism, and prestige.

Why Participate in the BCM Chess Tournament

🏆

Prestigious Competition

Engage in FIDE-rated divisions across Elite, Open, Ladies, and Youth (U-10 to U-16) categories.

🏛️

Elegant Venue, Professional Ambience

MICC is one of Zambia's top-tier conference venues, offering modern halls, AV technology, interpretation systems, and polished event execution.

📍

Prime Accessibility

Located in the heart of Lusaka, MICC provides easy access from hotels, shopping centers, and Kenneth Kaunda International Airport—less than a 30-minute drive away.

🏢

Comprehensive Facilities

Host to seven conference halls (including one with up to 2,500-delegate capacity), MICC offers ample parking, high-speed internet, catering, and attentive support.

⭐

Attested Quality

Visitors praise its spacious, well-maintained ambiance, top-notch tech setup, and professional staff—making it ideal for large-scale, high-energy events.

Central & Accessible

  • Prime Location: Situated on Great East Road in Lusaka, MICC is in close proximity to hotels, banks, shopping malls, and under a 30-minute drive from the airport.
  • Generous Parking: Offers a secure parking area accommodating approximately 650 vehicles, providing convenience and safety for all attendees.

Extensive Facilities

Event Spaces:

  • Seven adaptable conference halls, including a main hall with seating for 2,500 delegates
  • A suite of spaces including Main Auditorium, Banquet Hall, Committee Rooms, Meeting Rooms, Board Rooms

About BCM Consult

BCM Consult is dedicated to promoting chess in Zambia and Africa. We organize high-quality FIDE-rated tournaments to foster talent and competition. Our mission is to create opportunities for players of all levels to compete, grow, and connect through the game of chess.
